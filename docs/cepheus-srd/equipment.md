# Equipment

The following section lists common equipment that adventurers may want in a Cepheus Engine campaign. Technological level indicates the local technology required to manufacture something with the listed capabilities.

## Technology Levels

| TL | Era | Description |
|----|-----|------------|
| 0-1 | Primitive | Bronze/Iron age technology |
| 2-3 | Primitive | Renaissance to early industrial |
| 4-5 | Industrial | Industrial revolution, radio, internal combustion |
| 6 | Industrial | Fission power, advanced computing |
| 7 | Pre-Stellar | Reliable spaceflight, satellites |
| 8 | Pre-Stellar | System-wide spaceflight |
| 9 | Pre-Stellar | Gravity manipulation, early jump drives |
| 10 (A) | Early Stellar | Jump drive spaceflight |
| 11 (B) | Early Stellar | Primitive AI |
| 12 (C) | Average Stellar | Advanced weather control |
| 13 (D) | Average Stellar | Battle dress, high AI |
| 14 (E) | Average Stellar | Fusion weapons |
| 15+ | Advanced | Super-advanced technology |

## Currency

The **Credit (Cr)** is the standard unit of currency. Larger denominations include:
- KiloCredit (KCr) = 1,000 Credits
- MegaCredit (MCr) = 1,000,000 Credits

## Armor

Armor reduces damage taken from attacks. A hit with Effect 6+ always inflicts at least 1 point of damage regardless of armor.
