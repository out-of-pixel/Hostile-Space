## Key Topics Covered

### Character Creation
- Six characteristics (Strength, Dexterity, Endurance, Intelligence, Education, Social Standing)
- Background skills
- 24 career options
- Skill advancement
- Mustering out benefits

### Rules System
- 2D6 core mechanic
- Characteristic modifiers
- Difficulty system (-6 to +6)
- Skill checks
- Combat system with 6-second rounds
- Damage and injury

### Gameplay
- 60+ skills covering combat, technical, social, and science areas
- Comprehensive equipment lists
- Armor and weapons
- Vehicles and starships
- Technology levels 0-15+

### Combat
- Personal combat with stance and positioning
- Space combat for starships
- Initiative and action economy
- Damage types and effects
