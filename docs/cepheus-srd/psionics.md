# Psionics

Psionics represents the potential of the mind to interact directly with the universe through psychic abilities.

## Psionic Strength (PSI)

In addition to the six standard characteristics, characters may have a seventh characteristic called **Psionic Strength (Psi)**.

### Testing for Psionic Ability

To determine if a character has psionic ability:

1. Roll 2D6 (minimum 0, maximum 15 for most humans)
2. A result of 0 means no psionic potential
3. Results 1-15 indicate level of psionic ability
4. Some alien species automatically have psionics
5. Some are anti-psionic (cannot use psionics)

### Psionic Development

Characters with Psionic Strength:
- Can train in psionic skills
- Cost of training: same as other skills
- Start with Psi-0 if they begin with any psionic skill
- Can increase Psi through special training

## Psionic Talents

Psionic talents are innate abilities that manifest through training.

### Telepathy

Direct mind-to-mind communication:
- **Range:** Line of sight or known individual
- **Effect:** Send/receive thoughts
- **Limitations:** Requires willing contact initially

### Clairvoyance

Perceive distant locations or events:
- **Range:** 1 km + 1 km per Psi level
- **Effect:** See distant places visually
- **Time:** Takes several minutes to establish

### Telekinesis

Move objects with the mind:
- **Range:** Line of sight, up to 10 meters
- **Effect:** Lift/move objects up to 10 kg + Psi kg
- **Concentration:** Requires focus

### Precognition

Glimpse possible futures:
- **Range:** Seconds to days ahead
- **Effect:** See likely outcomes
- **Reliability:** Less accurate for distant futures

### Pyrokinesis

Control and manipulate fire:
- **Range:** Line of sight
- **Effect:** Start fires, control flames
- **Power:** 1D6 damage per Psi level

### Teleportation

Transport self or objects instantly:
- **Range:** Line of sight or known location
- **Effect:** Move instantly to location
- **Limitations:** High cost and risk

## Psionic Skills

Psionic abilities function as skills:
- **Telepathy** (Intelligence-based)
- **Clairvoyance** (Intelligence-based)
- **Telekinesis** (Dexterity-based)
- **Precognition** (Intelligence-based)
- **Pyrokinesis** (Willpower-based)
- **Teleportation** (Intelligence-based)

## Using Psionics

### Activation

To use a psionic ability:
1. Declare intent
2. Make skill check (2D6 + skill + characteristic DM + difficulty)
3. On success, ability activates
4. On failure, no effect (but may attract attention)

### Cost

Most psionic abilities cost **Endurance or Psi points** to use:
- Cost: 1-6 points depending on ability
- Fatigue results from overuse
- Character regains points with rest

### Difficulty

| Task | Difficulty |
|------|-----------|
| Contact willing mind | Easy (+4) |
| Contact unwilling mind | Difficult (-2) |
| Control small object | Routine (+2) |
| Control large object | Difficult (-2) |
| Sense distant location | Difficult (-2) |
| Sense unknown location | Very Difficult (-4) |

## Limits of Psionics

- **Distance:** Limited by psionic range
- **Power:** Limited by Psi characteristic
- **Duration:** Most abilities are temporary
- **Fatigue:** Overuse causes exhaustion
- **Resistance:** Some beings are naturally resistant
- **Technology:** Can be blocked by psionic shielding

## Psionic Encounters

Encountering psionics in the universe:

- **Psionic Institutions** - Schools and academies
- **Psionic Enforcement** - Officials who monitor psionics
- **Rogue Psions** - Criminals using abilities illegally
- **Alien Psionics** - Species with natural abilities
- **Psionic Artifacts** - Ancient technology enhancing psionics

## Campaign Considerations

### Balancing Psionics

Psionics can be powerful. Consider:
- Limiting access through availability
- Requiring rare training
- High training costs
- Natural resistance in population
- Technology interference
- Making training difficult

### House Rules

Referees may want to:
- Restrict which abilities are available
- Require Psi higher than normal to use abilities
- Increase costs/limitations
- Add new abilities specific to campaign
- Allow/disallow psionics entirely

## Learning Psionics

Characters with Psi potential can learn psionics through:

1. **Academy Training** - Rare psionic schools (months)
2. **Private Tutor** - Rare psion teacher (months to years)
3. **Self-Taught** - Through study and meditation (years)
4. **Innate Ability** - Some species have natural talents

### Training Time

Learning a new psionic skill:
- Takes as long as learning normal skills
- Requires teacher (usually)
- High cost (10,000+ Credits)
- Can be dangerous if untrained

## Psionic Side Effects

Overusing psionics can cause:
- Fatigue and exhaustion
- Mental strain (temporary Intelligence loss)
- Burnout (permanent Psi reduction)
- Psychological effects
- Headaches and nosebleeds
